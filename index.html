LMS — Platform Requirements

Feeds the Online Courses flow · drives the LMS Timeline decision (see Q6)

Needs

Must-have for MVP
  • Courses (catalog + structure)
  • Timers / activity tracking
  • Customer portals (learner accounts)
  • SCORM compatibility
  • Audio content support
  • Engaging system / modern UX
  • AI help (in-course assistant)
  • Quiz / test / exam builder
  • Certification holder
  • Hold "Next" button until X seconds
  • Video content support
  • Adapt to new training models / theory

Wants

Phase 2 / aspirational
  • Anti-cheat software
  • AR / VR capabilities
  • AI smart-glasses connection
Knowledge feed: Course content, AI recommendations, and certification context all source from the FTP Brain (Section 1).
Enrollment: Customer purchases on Shopify → auto-enrollment via LMS API (Section 3, swimlane B).

Open Questions — Decisions Needed

Workshop sticky notes · assign owners before build kicks off

Q1 · Identity
Where does the customer account live — monday.com, Website, LMS, or Shopify? Pick one source of truth.
Owner: TBD · blocks Sections 3, 4, 5
Q2 · Security
SSO strategy, integration auth, analytics stack, PII handling across systems.
Owner: IT · review with leadership
Q3 · Brain Scope
What does the website pull from FTP Brain vs. what stays internal-only?
Owner: Content + Eng
Q4 · AI Chatbot
Do we ship an AI chatbot on the public site at launch, or phase 2?
Owner: Product
Q5 · Forms & Marketing
Form flows → automated marketing (Mailchimp). What's MVP vs nice-to-have?
Owner: Marketing
Q6 · LMS Timeline
When does the new LMS (or current LMS API) need to be ready for cutover? See Section 6 for the full spec.
Owner: Operations
Q7 · Compliance Tracker
Build inside Admin Portal, inside LMS, or standalone? Who owns renewals?
Owner: Product + Ops
Q8 · Reporting
Single reporting layer that consumes from Shopify + LMS + monday + QBO?
Owner: Finance + IT
